The Golden Rule: Singular subject → singular verb (often ends in -s)

✅ The dog barks. | ✅ The dogs bark.

Tricky Pitfalls

  • Collective nouns (team, flock, army) → singular: The team is winning.
  • Indefinite pronouns (everyone, somebody, each) → singular: Everyone is here.
  • Interrupting phrases: The bag of apples is on the table. (subject = bag)

Past Perfect Trap

📅 By the time the bus arrived [past], Sarah had waited [past perfect] for 20 minutes.
