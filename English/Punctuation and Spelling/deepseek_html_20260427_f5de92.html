Homophones & Commonly Confused Words

✅ 40+ interactive questions • Instant check • Explanations • Substitution method
🔤 Substitution Method (exam strategy): Replace the word with a definition / test sentence.
✔ their / there / they’re → “their” (possessive); “there” (place); “they’re” (they are).
✔ your / you’re → “your” (belongs to you); “you’re” (you are).
✔ its / it’s → “its” (possessive, no apostrophe); “it’s” (it is).
✔ affect/effect → affect = verb (to influence); effect = noun (result).
