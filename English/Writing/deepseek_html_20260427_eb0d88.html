📌 GL CREATIVE WRITING FORMULA
✔ 5‑min plan: WHO? WHERE? PROBLEM? CLIMAX? 5 WOW words.
✔ Show, don't tell: “sweaty palms” → nervous.
✔ TiP ToP paragraphs: Time, Place, Topic, Person change = new line.
✔ Dialogue rule: New speaker = new line + punctuation INSIDE speech marks.
